FAQ
  • question_answerHow do I use this app?
    1. Click The Join Button
    2. install this app when prompted for ease of use(Optional)
    3. Add a Workspace with a Name,Encryption Level,Password and a Team(Optional)
    4. You can send and recieve Messages to your team and add Projects to this Workspace after entering it
    5. You can also add your Ideas to the Projects
  • question_answerWhat does it cost?
    Ideator-Enc is a fully Free to use Application.
